5 Landing Page Tweaks to Double Conversions

VSS CRO Team

Jan 10, 2026 • 5 min read

Traffic is expensive. If you're sending people to a landing page that doesn't convert, you're literally burning money. Whether you're running Google or Meta ads, your landing page is the "moment of truth." Here are 5 quick wins to improve your conversion rate (CRO) today.

1. Radical Clarity Above the Fold

Within 3 seconds of landing, a user should know exactly what you do, who you do it for, and what the next step is. This is a common bottleneck we find in our Growth Audits. If they have to scroll to understand your value prop, you've already lost them.

2. Social Proof Near the Button

Place a testimonial or a "Trusted by X brands" logo block right next to your primary CTA button. It reduces last-minute friction. See our Case Studies for examples of how this impacted lead quality.

3. Remove the Navigation

On a dedicated landing page, remove the header links. You want one goal: the conversion. Don't give them an "About Us" link to wander off to. This is especially true for topical search traffic where you want to keep the user focused.

4. Mobile-First Optimization

Over 70% of social traffic is mobile. If your form is hard to fill on a phone, your Meta Ads ROI will suffer. Use large tap targets and simplified layouts.
